## The Origin Saga

In the age before DAGKnight, the blockchain realm was ruled by a single chain of command. Blocks were forged one at a time, each waiting in line like soldiers marching through a narrow pass. The kingdoms were slow, the people restless, and the miners — those tireless forge-workers — wasted vast energies competing to add but a single stone to an ever-growing wall.

Then came the revelation: **What if blocks need not march single-file?**

## The Discovery of the DAG

The scholars of the Kaspa Citadel uncovered ancient mathematics — the **Directed Acyclic Graph** — a structure where many paths could flow forward simultaneously without ever looping back on themselves. They realized that blocks could be forged in parallel, referencing multiple predecessors, creating not a chain but a *web of truth*.

> *"The chain constrains. The DAG liberates. In parallel lies the power the realm has always sought."*
> — First Axiom of the DAGKnight Order

## The PHANTOM Protocol

But parallel creation brought chaos — how could the realm determine the true order of events when multiple blocks arrived at once? The answer came in the form of **PHANTOM**, a protocol that examines the *shape* of connections in the DAG to separate honest knights from adversaries.

PHANTOM observes which blocks reference which, building a picture of who saw whom. Honest blocks, created by true knights, naturally cluster together — they reference each other because they propagate quickly through the network. Adversarial blocks, forged in shadow, arrive late and disconnected.

## The DAGKnight Protocol

The ultimate evolution — **DAGKnight** itself — brought adaptive confirmation magic. Rather than assuming fixed network conditions (as lesser protocols must), DAGKnight *observes the DAG in real-time* and adjusts its confirmation threshold automatically.

The result: confirmations that are as fast as the network allows, with security guarantees that hold even against the most cunning adversaries.

### The Three Properties

- **Parallel Block Creation** — Multiple knights forge simultaneously, no wasted effort
- **Inclusive Ordering** — All honest blocks are included, none orphaned or abandoned
- **Adaptive Finality** — Confirmation speed adjusts to real conditions, not assumptions

## The Knights Today

The DAGKnight order continues to grow, building toward a future where the speed of thought meets the security of mathematics. Their quest: to make the BlockDAG the foundation of a new decentralized world.

The realm awaits those bold enough to join.
